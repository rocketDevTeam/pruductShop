What Is a Video Surveillance System?

The benefits of video surveillance systems include:

  • Additional Security: Many property owners who already own security systems invest in video surveillance for additional support. A traditional security system enables a property owner to receive instant notifications if a break-in occurs. Meanwhile, a video surveillance system allows a property owner to record real-time events on and around a property, providing additional insight into break-ins and other security issues.
  • Advanced Support: Smartphones and other mobile devices have transformed the global video surveillance marketplace. Today, mobile devices make it simple for property owners to review video footage of security incidents at their convenience. Video surveillance systems also enable property owners to receive up-to-the-minute details about what’s happening on and around a property.
  • Cost Savings: Video surveillance increases security and reduces risk. Many insurance companies offer business and home insurance discounts to those who install video surveillance systems. As a result, video surveillance systems sometimes help property owners reduce their business or home insurance costs.
  • Simple to Use: Many video surveillance systems are simple to use, ensuring both commercial and residential property owners can maximize the value of these systems.
